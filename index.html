TrentaFit

30 Day at Home Fitness Challenge

No fitness equipment? No time to go to the gym? No problem! TrentaFit allows you to workout at anytime, anywhere, using calisthenics and aerobic exercises.

Sick of following a pre-set fitness plan? TrentaFit allows you to be your own personal trainer! Customize the exercises in your workout, the starting reps, the daily increment, and how often you want to rest.

TrentaFit can help you lose weight, improve health, increase and tone muscle at home, without a gym, in just 30 short days.
